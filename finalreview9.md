# cancer
### free radical
* unstable oxygen molecules that have lost some of their electrons and are the product of radiation and chemicals
* oxygen becomes highly reactive when it loses some electrons and is unstable
* interacts w/ other oxygen to gain back the electrons
* termed reactive oxygen species
* many antioxidant enzymes repair damage done by free radicals
* excess free radicals cause damage
* may take 10-30 years to manifest into cancer
